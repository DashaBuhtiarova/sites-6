About the Artist

Rebecca Sugar is a distinguished American animator, composer, and screenwriter. She made history as the first woman to create her own show on Cartoon Network, the cult series «Steven Universe».

Her work is imbued with themes of empathy, love, and self-discovery. Before creating Steven Universe, Rebecca worked on Adventure Time, where her contributions to character development (especially Marceline) and songwriting earned her critical acclaim and the love of audiences worldwide.
